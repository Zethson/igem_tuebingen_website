The Dark Proteome

More than one third of the proteins in the human proteome are entirely disordered (IDPs) or contain long intrinsically disordered regions (IDRs).
They cannot be characterized by the traditional methods of structural biology and therefore pose an interesting challenge.
This website serves as an information source for the dark proteome.
