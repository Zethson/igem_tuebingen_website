Sponsoring

With the generous support of our sponsors it so far has always been possible to successfully take part in the iGEM competition. By financially supporting us and by providing us with materials, our sponsors have contributed greatly to our success. They are who enabled us to complete professional research projects on our own and to receive unique and outstanding training. Since we are a student-organized team responsible for organization, planning, funding and representation, participating in iGEM would otherwise not have been possible for us. While paving the way for a promising project our supporters could also connect with highly motivated and talented students. We are immensely grateful for the support of our sponsors and are looking forward to working with you!

Project 2019

The aim of the project 2019 is to generate a probiotic for the therapy of Type II Diabetes Mellitus, based on a plasmid chassis incorporating a Cas3 self-kill mechanism to prevent the spread of GMOs in the environment.
