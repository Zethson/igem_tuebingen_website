{{ _('About iGEM') }}

{{ _('iGEM (International Genetically Engineered Machine) is an international competition in the field of synthetic biology, which was initiated by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) in Boston. The competition aims to inspire the development of innovative and intelligent solutions for relevant and recent problems through genetic modifications. By participating in the iGEM competition, our team will be able to utilize the theoretical background attained during our studies in an interdisciplinary and self-reliant manner. Often, the projects initiated through iGEM are published or lead to the foundation of Start-ups.') }}
